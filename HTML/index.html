About Crafts by Malika

Crafts by Malika is a genuine family business based in Sri Lanka. Every piece is crocheted by hand by Malika, from soft amigurumi toys to shawls, scrunchies and home décor.

Because each item is handmade — and most are made to order — you can ask for different colours, sizes or small personal touches. No two handmade pieces are ever exactly alike, and that is part of what makes them special.

Frequently asked questions

Are the products really handmade?

Yes. Every item is crocheted by hand by Malika in Sri Lanka.

Where is Crafts by Malika based?

We are a small family business based in Sri Lanka.

Are items in stock or made to order?

Most items are made to order, and some décor pieces are one-of-a-kind. Message us about a specific item and we'll confirm its availability.

How long do custom orders take?

It depends on the item and how busy we are. Please ask when you enquire and we'll give you a timeframe.

Can I change the colours and sizes?

Yes — because everything is handmade, colours and sizes can usually be tailored. Just ask.

Is local pickup available?

Please contact us to arrange this — we'll confirm what's possible.

Where do you deliver in Sri Lanka, and do you ship internationally?

We're happy to discuss delivery within Sri Lanka and internationally. Contact us with your location and we'll confirm options and costs.

Which currency will I be charged in, and why might the amount differ?

Our prices are set in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). If you pay from another country, your bank or the payment provider converts the amount and may add a conversion fee, so the total in your own currency can vary slightly. We'll always confirm the final amount with you before you pay.

How should I care for crochet items?

As a general guide, gentle hand washing and drying flat is safest for handmade crochet. We'll include specific care notes with your item.

Are the toys suitable for young children?

Please ask us about a specific toy before buying for a young child — we'll tell you about materials and any small parts so you can decide.

What is your return policy?

Our returns policy is being finalised. For now, please contact us if there's any problem with your order and we'll do our best to help.
