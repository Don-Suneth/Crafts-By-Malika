Policies & information

Plain-English information about ordering from Crafts by Malika. Some details below are still being finalised by the business — where that's the case it is clearly marked, and you are welcome to contact us to confirm anything before you order.

Shipping & delivery

We are based in Sri Lanka and can discuss both local delivery and international shipping. Because most items are handmade to order, we confirm the delivery method, timeframe and cost with you before you pay.

Being finalised: domestic delivery regions and prices, courier partners, international shipping availability and how it is calculated, and any free-shipping thresholds. Please contact us for a shipping quote in the meantime.

Customs & import duties: for international orders, any import duties or taxes charged by the destination country are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our prices.

Returns & refunds

Each piece is made by hand and checked before it is sent. If something arrives damaged or not as described, please contact us promptly with photos and we'll work with you to put it right.

Being finalised: the formal returns window and refund/exchange terms. Please note that custom and made-to-order items are commonly non-returnable unless faulty. We'll confirm the exact terms before this is published.

Handmade variation

Everything we sell is crocheted by hand, so small variations in size, colour and finish are natural and expected — no two pieces are ever identical. Yarn colours may also look slightly different on your screen compared with the real item. These characteristics are part of what makes handmade work special and are not considered faults.

Product care

As a general guide, hand wash crochet items gently in cool water and dry them flat to keep their shape. Avoid wringing, tumble drying or harsh detergents.

Being finalised: specific care instructions per item once the exact yarns are confirmed. Care notes will be included with each order.

Toy & small-parts safety

Some toys may include small parts or fixed safety eyes. If you are buying for a baby or young child, please ask us about a specific item first so we can confirm the materials and whether it is suitable, or make a baby-safe version.

Being finalised: confirmed age guidance and whether each toy uses embroidered features or safety eyes. We will not make a safety claim until this is confirmed.

Custom-order terms

For custom and made-to-order pieces, we confirm the design, size, colours, price, currency and timeframe with you in writing before work begins. Because these items are made specifically for you, they may not be returnable unless faulty. Any deposit or payment terms will be agreed with you up front.

Currency & pricing

Our prices are set and shown in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR), our base currency. We do not display automatic converted prices in other currencies, because exchange rates change constantly and we don't want to show you a figure that turns out to be inaccurate.

If you are ordering from outside Sri Lanka, the amount in your own currency is determined by your bank or the payment provider at the time you pay, and they may add a conversion fee. In other words:

Converted prices are estimates. Your payment provider or card issuer will confirm the final amount and may apply conversion fees.

We will always confirm the total with you before you pay. See PAYMENTS_AND_CURRENCY.md in the project for the full approach.

Frequently asked questions

Are the products really handmade?

Yes. Every item is crocheted by hand by Malika in Sri Lanka.

Where is Crafts by Malika based?

We are a small family business based in Sri Lanka.

Are items in stock or made to order?

Most items are made to order, and some décor pieces are one-of-a-kind. Message us about a specific item and we'll confirm its availability.

How long do custom orders take?

It depends on the item and how busy we are. Please ask when you enquire and we'll give you a timeframe.

Can I change the colours and sizes?

Yes — because everything is handmade, colours and sizes can usually be tailored. Just ask.

Is local pickup available?

Please contact us to arrange this — we'll confirm what's possible.

Where do you deliver in Sri Lanka, and do you ship internationally?

We're happy to discuss delivery within Sri Lanka and internationally. Contact us with your location and we'll confirm options and costs.

Which currency will I be charged in, and why might the amount differ?

Our prices are set in Sri Lankan Rupees (LKR). If you pay from another country, your bank or the payment provider converts the amount and may add a conversion fee, so the total in your own currency can vary slightly. We'll always confirm the final amount with you before you pay.

How should I care for crochet items?

As a general guide, gentle hand washing and drying flat is safest for handmade crochet. We'll include specific care notes with your item.

Are the toys suitable for young children?

Please ask us about a specific toy before buying for a young child — we'll tell you about materials and any small parts so you can decide.

What is your return policy?

Our returns policy is being finalised. For now, please contact us if there's any problem with your order and we'll do our best to help.
