Information

Crafts by Malika is a small family business in Sri Lanka making handmade crochet. The pieces shown on this site are representative examples of the kind of work we make — colours, sizes, availability and details are arranged with you when you enquire. You're welcome to contact us with any questions before you decide.

How ordering works

The products on the homepage are examples of our handmade work, not a stocked catalogue. To order, send us an enquiry describing what you'd like, and we'll take it from there.

  • We confirm whether a piece is available, and how it can be customised, after we receive your enquiry.
  • We confirm the final price with you before you commit to anything.
  • We confirm the production timeframe after discussing the details of your request.

Delivery

We discuss delivery arrangements and costs with you directly as part of your enquiry, based on the item and where it needs to go. Any delivery cost is confirmed with you before you commit to the order.

Pricing & payment

Prices depend on the item, the materials and any customisation, so we give you a price once we understand your request. The final price is always confirmed with you before you commit to the order.

Payment arrangements are agreed directly with you after you receive and accept the quote.

This website does not process cards or collect any payment information. Nothing is charged through the site.

Handmade variation

Every piece is crocheted by hand, so small variations in size, colour and finish are natural and expected — no two pieces are ever exactly alike. Yarn colours may also look slightly different on screen compared with the finished item. These characteristics are part of handmade work and are not faults.

Damaged or incorrect items

Each piece is checked by hand before it is sent. If your item arrives damaged or is not what we agreed, please contact us promptly — ideally with photos — and we'll work with you to put it right.

Custom orders

For custom and made-to-order pieces, we confirm the design, size, colours, price and timeframe with you before work begins, so you know what to expect. Because these items are made specifically for you, please check the details carefully when we discuss them.

Frequently asked questions

Are the products really handmade?

Yes. Every item is crocheted by hand by Malika in Sri Lanka.

Are the products on the homepage in stock?

They are examples of our handmade work rather than stocked items. Contact us about a piece and we'll confirm what's possible.

Can I change the colours and sizes?

Usually, yes — because everything is handmade, colours and sizes can often be tailored. Tell us what you have in mind and we'll confirm.

How much does an item cost?

Prices depend on the item and any customisation. We confirm the price with you before you commit to the order.

How long will my order take?

It depends on the piece and how busy we are. We confirm a timeframe with you after discussing your request.

How do I pay?

Payment is arranged directly with you after you accept the quote. The website itself does not take card or payment details.

Are the toys suitable for young children?

Please ask us about a specific item before buying for a young child, and we'll tell you about the materials and any small parts so you can decide.

Privacy

When you send an enquiry, we collect only the details you choose to give us — such as your name, contact details and what you'd like made. We use this information only to respond to you and to manage the order you've asked about.

  • We don't sell or share your details for marketing.
  • Form submissions are processed by our form provider, Formspree, which handles your message so it can reach us by email.
  • For any privacy question, or to ask us to delete your enquiry details, email craftsbymalika@gmail.com.
