# Business Setup Checklist — Crafts by Malika

A short operational checklist for keeping the site accurate and replying to customers.
**Tick an item only once you have actually confirmed it** — leave anything unconfirmed
unticked. Do not state invented facts (stock, delivery promises, return windows,
prices, payment methods or safety claims).

Most edits are in `apps/website/config.js` (business details) and
`apps/website/products.js` (the three examples).

## Identity & contact
- [ ] Public business name shown is correct: **Crafts by Malika**.
- [ ] Business email is correct and monitored: **craftsbymalika@gmail.com**.
- [ ] Facebook link is correct: <https://www.facebook.com/crafts.bymalika>
- [ ] Instagram link is correct: <https://www.instagram.com/crafts.bymalika>
- [ ] Public location wording is acceptable (currently "Sri Lanka" — keep it a
      region/country, not a home address).

      **Formspree** inbox for `craftsbymalika@gmail.com`.
- [ ] Agree a simple reply process: who replies to enquiries, and roughly how quickly.
